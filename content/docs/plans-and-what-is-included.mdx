Synq has three plans: Starter, Pro and Scale. Every plan starts with a 7-day free trial, so you can see the value before you pay. All plans price your cards from the market and let you re-price in one click; Pro and Scale add hands-off automation.

## What is included in every plan?

Every plan, from Starter up, includes:

- Add cards and create products from your [templates](/help/product-templates).
- Price every card from live market data with your [strategy](/help/how-synq-prices-your-cards) and [price rules](/help/price-rules).
- [Re-price your store in one click](/help/keep-prices-current) with Sync all prices.

## What is different between plans?

- **Product limit**: Starter up to 2,000 cards, Pro up to 15,000, Scale up to 50,000.
- **Automatic daily repricing**: included on **Pro and Scale**. The [repricing run](/help/keep-prices-current) updates your prices once a day at an hour you choose, with no clicking. Starter keeps prices current with the one-click Sync all prices button.

For the current prices and exact limits, see the [pricing page](/pricing).

## When should I move up?

Step up a plan when:

- You are approaching your **product limit** and need to list more cards.
- You want **automatic repricing** so prices stay current without clicking (Pro or Scale).
- The market moves often and you want prices always up to date.

## How do I change plans?

Manage your plan from within the Synq app. Changes take effect on your store right away.
