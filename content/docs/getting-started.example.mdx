## Welcome to Synq

Synq is a Shopify app that automatically keeps your trading card prices in sync with TCGPlayer market data. Once installed, you can add cards to your store in one click and let Synq handle daily price updates.

## Installation

1. Visit the [Synq listing on the Shopify App Store](https://apps.shopify.com/synq-tcg-card-manager)
2. Click **Add app** and follow the OAuth flow to authorize Synq on your store
3. Once installed, you'll be redirected to the Synq dashboard

## First-time setup

After installation, Synq walks you through a short setup flow:

1. **Connect TCGPlayer** — Authorize Synq to read TCGPlayer market prices for your region
2. **Choose your games** — Select which TCG games you carry (MTG, Pokémon, Lorcana, etc.)
3. **Set a pricing strategy** — Pick a default multiplier (e.g. 100% of market price, 110%, etc.)
4. **Run your first sync** — Synq will pull the latest prices and apply them to your inventory

## Adding your first cards

Once setup is complete, head to **Cards → Add Cards**. Search by card name, set, or collector number. When you find the card, click **Add to Store** — Synq creates the Shopify product and maps it to the TCGPlayer price feed automatically.

## What happens next

Every day, Synq runs a price sync in the background. Cards whose market price has changed by more than your configured threshold will have their Shopify price updated automatically. You can review the sync log in **Activity → Sync History**.
