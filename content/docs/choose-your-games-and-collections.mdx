Your catalog settings tell Synq which games you sell and where new products belong. Getting this right means every card you add lands in the correct Shopify collection automatically.

## How do I choose my games?

Open **Settings, then Catalog** in Synq and select the games you sell. Synq supports the major trading card games, including Magic: The Gathering, Pokemon, Disney Lorcana, Yu-Gi-Oh!, One Piece and more. This selection tells Synq which catalogs to search and price, and you can change it at any time.

## How do collections work?

For each game, you can set:

- A **singles collection**, where individual cards land.
- A **sealed collection**, where booster boxes and other sealed product land.

When you [add a product](/help/adding-products-to-your-store), Synq puts it in the matching collection based on the game and whether it is a single or [sealed](/help/adding-products-to-your-store).

## Why split singles and sealed?

Buyers shop them differently. Separate collections let you merchandise booster boxes apart from singles, and they pair well with [separate templates](/help/product-templates) so each one gets its own listing format.

## What is next?

- Set the **brand** shown on products with [vendors](/help/product-templates).
- Control where products publish with [sales channels and auto-publish](/help/publishing-and-sales-channels).
